Register to Bid

Registration is required before bidding. Stripe securely saves your payment method. If you are the highest bidder when an auction ends, your saved payment method is automatically charged for your winning bid plus estimated shipping and packaging.

Important: Your card is not charged when you register. It is only charged automatically if you win an auction. Email is required so you can receive registration confirmation and receipts.

Shipping: Each artwork page displays the estimated shipping/packaging charge before you bid. If you win, your saved payment method is charged for the winning bid plus that displayed shipping/packaging estimate.
