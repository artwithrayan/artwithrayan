Shipping Policy

Original artwork shipping is charged using an estimated shipping and packaging amount shown before bidding. This estimate may include packaging materials, protective supplies, carrier buffer, and handling.

Print orders are fulfilled through Printful when enabled. Original artwork is packed and shipped manually by Rayan Rao Art.
