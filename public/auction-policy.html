Auction Policy

Bids are binding offers to purchase the artwork if the bidder wins. Registered bidders authorize Rayan Rao Art to charge the saved Stripe payment method for the winning bid and the displayed shipping and packaging charge.

If a charge fails, Rayan Rao Art may contact the bidder, cancel the sale, charge the second-highest bidder, or relist the artwork.

Shipping estimates are calculated from artwork size, package requirements, materials, and handling.
